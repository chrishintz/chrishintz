My Story

I was born in Bellevue, Washington. I lived the first 18 years of my life in the same house which meant by the end of high school I was ready to try out another part of the country and moved to Atlanta, GA to attend Emory University and study Accounting and Finance.

After college, I moved to Denver, Colorado to work as an auditor for PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC), working in the Energy, Mining, and Real Estate industries. After three years, my westward journey continued as I moved to San Jose, California to work in PwC's Transaction Services, Financial Due Diligence group focusing on technology companies. While in the Bay Area, I found myself more intrested in the technology side of the businesses I worked on rather than the accounting/finance side.

After one year in the Bay Area, I returned home to Seattle, continuing to work with PwC as an outside consultant before joining Code Builders to train to become a developer. In my free time, I enjoys hiking and biking around the beautiful Pacific Northwest with my girlfriend, Calla, and my dog, Pawpea.
